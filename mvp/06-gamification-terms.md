# Gamification Terms — Workshop

## Goal

Make older online volunteers feel they **contribute to society** — not grind for meaningless points.

## Naming options (pick one for beta)

| Option | Points | Rank ladder | Vibe |
|--------|--------|-------------|------|
| **A — recommended** | **True Trini Points (TTP)** | Watcher → Sentinel → WiTTness Sentinel | Local pride; matches brand |
| **B** | Witness Points (WP) | Observer → Witness → Lead Witness | Neutral; works abroad |
| ~~GAP~~ | ~~Guardian Angel Points~~ | — | **Avoid** — conflicts with Guardian Media, Guardian Life, etc. in T&T |

**Open:** Is "macco" too pejorative for public branding, or usable ironically?

## Point events (MVP)

| Action | Points (relative) |
|--------|-------------------|
| Delivery confirm (within cap) | 10 base + early bonus |
| Legitimate overdue escalate (admin affirmed) | 3 |
| Legitimate urgent concern (admin affirmed) | 5 |
| Frivolous escalate overturned | −10 + strike |
| Surge day verified flags | Badge only |

**Rule:** Escalate points **<** confirm points — avoid escalate-chasing.

## Ranks and trust levels

| Trust | Name (workshop) | Unlock |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| 0 | New | View + confirm |
| 1 | Watcher | Escalate overdue |
| 2 | Sentinel | Urgent concern; limited mod queue |
| 3 | Lead Sentinel | Ban, resolve escalations |

Promotion: accuracy + tenure — not volume alone.

## Badges

- Parish champion (most confirms in week)
- Rural ally (confirms on rural trips)
- Surge Sentinel (bad day — 10+ admin-affirmed flags)
- Zero false escalation streak (30 days)

## Circuit mode

Leaderboard **frozen**; message: *"Support mode — witness only."*
