# Current Mitigations — What Exists Today

## Parent and community practices

| Practice | What it helps | What it fails to do |
|----------|---------------|---------------------|
| Photo of car, driver, plates | After-the-fact ID; deterrent if driver knows | No live trip state; no deviation alert; no driver protection |
| Word-of-mouth trusted drivers | Reduces unknown risk | Does not scale; fails when trusted driver unavailable |
| Share live location (WhatsApp) | Real-time dot on map | Child needs phone; driver can be unrelated to dot; privacy leak |
| Avoid hire entirely | Eliminates hire risk | Strands families without cars — especially rural |
| Police reports after harm | Justice pathway | Reactive only |

## Driver practices

| Practice | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Refuse unknown detours | Income loss; passenger retaliation; no platform backing |
| Dashcam | Evidence after; not prevention |
| Avoid certain areas/time | Reduces earnings |

## Institutional

- Hire-car **permitting** exists but many trips are informal.
- Travel advisories warn about taxi/ride safety — reputational damage at national level.

## Gap the project fills

```
Identification (photo)  →  AFTER harm
Live trip state         →  MISSING
Remote witness quorum   →  MISSING
Driver detour protection →  MISSING
Rural-aware escalation  →  MISSING
```

The MVP does not solve all gaps; it introduces **live trip state + remote witness quorum + escalation** as the first wedge.
