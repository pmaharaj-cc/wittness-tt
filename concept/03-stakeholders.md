# Stakeholders

## Primary

### Parents and guardians

- Register trips; confirm start and end (except pre-authorized school runs).
- Hold the **panic button** authority.
- Often **no private car** — especially rural; hire is not optional.
- Need **low friction** (SMS, WhatsApp, web — not mandatory app install for week 1).

### Children

- May have **no smartphone**.
- Sync via verbal trip code, guardian proxy, or future simple token — see [`07-initialization-tiers.md`](07-initialization-tiers.md).

### Drivers

- Opt-in verified (permit-linked) vs **anonymous** (plate only).
- Need protection from **coerced detours** and robbery — longer-term thread.
- May disable tracking; design cannot assume GPS compliance.

### Community monitors / Guardians

- Target demographic: **older chronically online users**, especially women at home during the day.
- Motivated by **contribution**, rank, parish pride, shareable credentials.
- **Remote witnesses** — not emergency responders.

## Secondary

### Schools

- **Must not** be burdened with daily app checks or bell-time workflows — see [`../mvp/03-parent-confirmation.md`](../mvp/03-parent-confirmation.md).

### Transport Board / regulators

- Permit verification for trusted driver tier (future).

### TTPS / emergency services

- **999** for immediate danger; platform does not dispatch police in MVP.

### Founders and Sentinel admins

- Moderation, triage on bad days, abuse response.

## Anti-stakeholders

- Bad actors farming panics or escalations to crash the system
- Doxxers using trip data to harass drivers or families
- Vigilante mobs bypassing sanitization
